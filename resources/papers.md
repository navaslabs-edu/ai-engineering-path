# Papers Worth Reading

You don't need to understand every equation on the first read. Focus on: the problem the paper solves, the approach it proposes, and why it mattered. Treat this as ongoing enrichment alongside the main path, not a gate.

1. **Attention Is All You Need** — the Transformer paper. The foundation everything from Session 06 onward builds on.
2. **BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding**
3. **GPT-3: Language Models are Few-Shot Learners**
4. **Retrieval-Augmented Generation for Knowledge-Intensive NLP Tasks** — read this alongside Sessions 08–13.
5. **LoRA: Low-Rank Adaptation of Large Language Models**
6. **QLoRA: Efficient Finetuning of Quantized LLMs**
